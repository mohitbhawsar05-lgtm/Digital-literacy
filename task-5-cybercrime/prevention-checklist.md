STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages
- Always check website URLs before entering details
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal information

(UPI / Financial Safety Tips)
- Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Always verify the receiver before sending money
- Avoid scanning random QR codes

(Reporting Channels)
- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number: 1930 immediately for fraud cases
